  • Intake for Computer Science and Electronics Communication Engineering increased by 30 (from 90 to 120).

  • Obtained ISO 9001:2008 Certification from United Registrar of Systems.

1982 - 1983

The institute started operations with three basic branches: Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical & Electronics Engineering. Engineering branches began with an intake of 40 students each at the Bangaramma Sajjan Campus.
