Placement Training - Comprehensive Approach

Phase-Wise Training Modules

The Placement Cell provides structured training through a phased approach to bridge the gap between industry expectations and academic knowledge.

Phase 1 (Second Year): Focuses on foundational skills, including life skills, communication, and essential soft skills, to prepare students for advanced learning.

Phase 2 (Third Year): Provides aptitude training, reading skills enhancement, and problem-solving techniques to build analytical and logical thinking abilities.

Phase 3 (Fourth Year): Offers technical training, group discussion strategies, personal interview preparation, and career-specific guidance to ensure job readiness.

Industry Exposure

In addition to the training modules, the Placement Cell emphasizes real-world exposure through collaborations with industry leaders. The Infosys Campus Connect Program provides students with hands-on industry insights, where experts deliver talks and inspire students with their experience.

Such initiatives bridge the academic-industry gap and motivate students to align their skills with market demands.

Importance of Placement Training

Securing a position in top organizations is the dream of every engineering graduate. However, in today’s competitive job market, it is essential to possess not just technical skills but also strong interpersonal abilities. Recognizing this, the institution ensures:

  • Enhanced employability skills through life skills training.
  • Focus on building well-rounded personalities, essential for professional success.
  • Comprehensive development to meet industry standards.

Training Objectives

The placement training programs are designed to achieve the following objectives:

  • 100% Employment: Ensuring all students secure meaningful opportunities.
  • Core Competencies: Identifying and developing students' key strengths.
  • Career Development: Providing training that aligns with industry expectations.
  • Confidence Building: Instilling self-belief and a positive attitude.
  • Communication Skills: Enhancing verbal and written communication for professional settings.

Additional Training Activities

The Placement Cell also conducts supplementary programs to ensure holistic preparation:

  • Awareness workshops on career planning and mapping.
  • Personality development sessions to boost self-presentation skills.
  • Quantitative, logical, and verbal reasoning training through expert trainers.
  • Mock interviews to simulate real-world recruitment scenarios.
  • Group discussion practice for collaborative communication improvement.

Assessment & Feedback

The Placement Cell evaluates students’ progress through various methods:

  • Online tests to measure aptitude and problem-solving skills.
  • Written tests for assessing analytical capabilities.
  • Regular feedback sessions to identify improvement areas.

These efforts ensure students are fully prepared for their professional journeys.
