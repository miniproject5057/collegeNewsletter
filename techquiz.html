IEEE Week - TechKnowLogist Quiz Competition

Welcome to the IEEE Student Branch STB30721 at V.P. Dr. PG Hallakatti College of Engineering & Technology! This year, we are thrilled to host an exciting lineup of events during our annual IEEE Week, from October 1 to October 5. Among these, the highlight of Day 3 is the TechKnowLogist Quiz Competition, an event designed to challenge and inspire students across technical and intellectual domains.

The TechKnowLogist Quiz Competition isn’t just another quiz; it’s a platform for students to showcase their general knowledge, technical expertise, and problem-solving abilities. Spanning across three rounds, the quiz is structured to ensure fair competition and a fun learning experience for all participants.

Quiz Structure and Rounds

The competition is divided into the following three rounds, each progressively challenging and engaging:

  • Round 1: Written Assessment
    Participants will tackle a set of 50 questions, split into:
    • 25 Multiple Choice Questions (General Knowledge)
    • 15 Fill-in-the-Blank Questions
    • 10 Aptitude Questions
    Teams must score at least 20 points to proceed to the next round.
  • Round 2: Rapid Fire Round
    Teams will be presented with visuals such as company logos and slogans. They need to correctly identify at least 12 visuals to qualify for the final round.
  • Round 3: Buzzer Round
    In this thrilling final round, teams will answer quick-response questions related to general and technical knowledge. Scoring is as follows:
    • Correct Answer: +5 points
    • Incorrect Answer: -2 points
    The team with the highest score will emerge as the champion.

Prizes and Participation

The quiz competition offers not just a chance to compete but also exciting rewards:

  • Winning Team: ₹1200/- and certificates
  • Runner-Up Team: ₹600/- and certificates
  • Participation: E-certificates for all participants

Registration fees are set at ₹25 for IEEE members and ₹70 for non-IEEE members, making the event accessible to all.
