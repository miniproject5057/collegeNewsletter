Other Facilities at BLDEACET

ATM Convenience

To provide our students and staff with hassle-free access to banking services, we have collaborated with the State Bank of India to install Automated Teller Machines (ATMs) within the campus. These ATMs allow individuals to conduct a range of financial transactions, including cash withdrawals, balance inquiries, and more, without having to step outside the campus premises. This service saves time and ensures that financial needs are met conveniently and securely.

Green Campus Oasis

At BLDEACET, we are deeply committed to environmental sustainability and creating a healthy space for our students and faculty. Our campus is a haven of greenery, with vast expanses of manicured lawns, flourishing flowerbeds, and tree-lined walkways. The pollution-free environment not only promotes physical well-being but also provides a tranquil and inspiring setting for academic pursuits. By maintaining eco-friendly practices, such as waste segregation and water conservation, we foster a culture of environmental responsibility.

Uninterrupted Water & Electricity

Ensuring a seamless campus experience is a top priority at BLDEACET. Our facilities are designed to provide uninterrupted water and electricity supply, so students can focus on their academic goals without distractions. Water coolers equipped with advanced filtration systems are strategically placed to guarantee access to clean and safe drinking water at all times. Additionally, our campus is equipped with high-capacity generators that activate during power outages, ensuring uninterrupted operations. We adhere to stringent standards of cleanliness and hygiene, making the campus a comfortable and reliable environment.

Health & Well-being Initiatives

Recognizing the critical connection between health and academic performance, BLDEACET actively promotes the well-being of its students and staff. Regular health camps are conducted on campus, offering comprehensive health check-ups and consultations at no cost. These initiatives aim to raise awareness about preventive healthcare and encourage individuals to adopt healthier lifestyles. From nutrition guidance to mental health resources, our programs address diverse aspects of well-being, empowering our community to thrive both academically and personally.

Medical Facilities

We place the utmost importance on the health and safety of our students. Each hostel is equipped with first aid kits to handle minor injuries and emergencies promptly. For more serious medical concerns, our campus is located close to highly qualified physicians who offer consultancy services when required. During working hours, students have access to free first aid assistance, ensuring immediate attention to their medical needs. These facilities are part of our broader commitment to creating a supportive and secure environment for all members of the BLDEACET family.
